Martin's Fish Aquarium
Carp: Carp have been found in the tailwaters of Taylorsville Lake
Black Bass: Cast a line into any lake, river, or ornamental pond in the Lower 48 and you're likely to pull out a black bass
Crappie: Crappie will spawn in the major creeks and sloughs
Catfish: This southern delicacy is raised in earthen ponds filled with well water and fed a floating, grain-based diet.
Bull Shark: warm waters, and can travel from the ocean into rivers and lakes.
Swordfish: tropical, temperate, and sometimes cold waters of the Atlantic, Indian, and Pacific Oceans
*IMPORTANT* Maintain aquarium water quality by testing early and often; Avoid over cleaning to protect your good bacteria; Conduct a partial aquarium water change regularly
